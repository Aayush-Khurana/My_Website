Introduction

Hello I’m Aayush Khurana and I’m a student at LBS pursuing a Masters in Financial Analysis. Prior to joining LBS, I was at King’s College London where I pursued my undergradate degree in Economics and Management. During my time at KCL, I was heavily involved in several societies namely -

  1. Enactus KCL as a Finance director
  2. King’s Global Markets as a VP
  3. the Squash team representing the KCL Men’s team in tournaments.

Previously, I have worked at EY as a business consultant and at ING bank as an equity research analyst. To further enhance my understanding of Finance, I also recently worked as a Corporate Finance analyst at a US based Fintech - [EdgeConneX.] (https://www.edgeconnex.com/)

Multi-cultural background

Since an early age, I have been fortunate enough to interact with people from varied backgrounds and cultures.
